Imagery

We all have heard the saying If you can dream it, you can do it. Well, psychological studies have proven this to be true, at least in relation to sports. Follow the page on imagery to learn about the research and applications of this powerful skill.


Concentration

The average human thinks about 4000 thoughts in a sixteen-hour day. Yet to do the very bestyou possibly could, it is necessary to eliminate distractions and focus fully on the task at hand. Read more about what science has to say about concentration.


Self-Confidence

Ambition powers our desire to achieve our goals in sports, but without believing that we could actually do it, we'd never get anywhere. People with low self-confidence perform poorly compared to people with higher levels of self-efficacy (another term for self-confidence). Get the latest info on self-confidence.
